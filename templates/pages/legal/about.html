About StockGame

What is StockGame?

StockGame is a fantasy stock market game where you compete with friends to build the best portfolio. You start with $1,000,000 in virtual cash and invest in real stocks from the ASX 200 and S&P 500.

Track real market prices, make trades, and see who can earn the highest returns — all without risking a cent of real money.

How to Play

  1. Sign in with your Google account
  2. Join or create a game — set the duration, starting balance, and which markets to trade
  3. Invite friends — share an invite link so others can join
  4. Browse stocks — search ASX and S&P 500 companies
  5. Buy & sell — execute trades using real-time market prices
  6. Track your portfolio — watch your holdings, returns, and rankings
  7. Win! — the player with the highest portfolio value at the end wins

Features

  • Real stock prices from ASX 200 and S&P 500
  • Limit orders — set a price target and your order fills automatically
  • Watchlists — track stocks you’re interested in
  • Leaderboards — see how you rank against other players
  • In-game chat — discuss strategy with other players
  • Achievements — earn badges for milestones like your first trade or a 25% return
  • Analytics — sector breakdown, trade stats, and portfolio charts
  • Multi-currency — trade in both AUD and USD

Important

StockGame is for entertainment and education only. No real money is involved. This is not a financial product and we do not provide investment advice. See our Terms of Service for details.
